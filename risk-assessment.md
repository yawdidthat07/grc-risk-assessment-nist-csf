# Security Risk Assessment Overview

## Scope
This assessment focuses on governance and operational security risks
within a regulated organization, with emphasis on access control,
policy enforcement, and audit readiness.

## Framework
- NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)
  - Identify
  - Protect
  - Detect
  - Respond
  - Recover

## Risk Identification Approach
Risks were identified through:
- Review of internal policies and procedures
- Evaluation of access management and governance controls
- Consideration of regulatory and compliance requirements

## Risk Evaluation
Each identified risk was assessed using:
- Likelihood (Low / Medium / High)
- Impact (Low / Medium / High)
- Overall risk rating based on combined score

## Outcomes
The assessment resulted in documented risks with
recommended mitigation actions and mapped controls
to support governance and compliance objectives.
